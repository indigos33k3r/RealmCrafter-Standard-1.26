PlaySpeech



Usage: 

		PlaySpeech(Actor, Speech)



Description:
The PlaySpeech command plays an actor speech sound to all players within hearing range. The Speech parameter should be one of the following:

0 - Greeting 1
1 - Greeting 2
2 - Goodbye 1
3 - Goodbye 2
4 - Attack 1
5 - Attack 2
6 - Hit 1
7 - Hit 2
8 - Request help
9 - Death 1
10 - Footstep (dry)
11 - Footstep (wet)



Example: 

		Function Main()
			; Say hello
			PlaySpeech(Actor(), Rand(0, 1))
			Return()
		End Function
