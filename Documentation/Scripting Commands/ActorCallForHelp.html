ActorCallForHelp



Usage: 

		ActorCallForHelp(Actor)



Description:
The ActorCallForHelp command causes all NPCs with a faction rating of over 90% with the specified actor's faction, who are also within range of the actor, to join in attacking the actor's target. This creates the illusion of social behaviour from NPCs. The range of the call is determined by the aggressiveness range of the NPCs being called. Pets and characters already involved in combat will not respond, nor will any characters with a non-combatant or passive aggression mode.



Example: 

		Function Main()
			; Make NPC call for help against its target
			ActorCallForHelp(ContextActor())
			Return()
		End Function
