ChangeFactionRating



Usage: 

		ChangeFactionRating(Actor, FactionName, Amount)



Description:
The ChangeFactionRating command modifies an actor's rating with a particular faction. You cannot cause a faction rating to go above 100 or below -100.



Example: 

		Function Main()
			ChangeFactionRating(Actor(), "Elves", 10)
			Output(Actor(), "Added 10 to your popularity with elves.")
			Return()
		End Function
